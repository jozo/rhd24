PLATO'S CAVE: With Philip-Neri Reese, O.P.

In-depth Platonic dialogue with the American Dominican philosopher on his life journey and search for God

Pridať do kalendára
Philip-Neri Reese, O.P.
Pridať do kalendára
No items found.
Get the ticket for the event

Event description:

Annotation:

Although Father Philip-Neri is a Dominican, Thomas Aquinas is certainly not his only subject. We'll talk about his life before he became a priest, his journey to the Dominicans, or how one can combine an interest in Thomism with Indian philosophy in a friendly dialogue about God, the universe, and life in general. By the way, which theologians does Philip-Neri find inspiring and which would he most like to put on the index? The format of the Plan Cave wants to give participants the experience of live contact with the guest and the opportunity for real dialogue. The number of places is therefore limited to 30.

Topics of the discussion:

  1. How did an American boy with the name of an Oratorian saint become a Dominican?
  2. What was his journey across American universities to Rome like?
  3. What does he consider the most precious thing in his life, the one thing he can't give up?
