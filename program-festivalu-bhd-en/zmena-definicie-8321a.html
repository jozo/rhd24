Redefining marriage - Why not?

A lecture by a Princeton professor on the negative consequences of changing your understanding of marriage that will change everything you've ever thought about the subject.

Pridať do kalendára
Robert P. George
Pridať do kalendára
Moderator:
Martin Luterán
Get the ticket for the event

Event description:

Annotation:

In Slovakia, unlike in many Western countries, marriage has not yet undergone an institutional change. Proponents of the change argue that extending marriage to same-sex couples will not fundamentally change anything, only expand freedom where it has been denied. But is this really the case? Professor George, one of the co-authors of the important book What is Marriage? (2012) argues that changing marriage will change just about everything. A groundbreaking lecture that has the potential to turn the current Slovak debate on its head...

‍

Lecture topics:

  1. What is marriage?
  2. Why is it the most important social institution?
  3. What would changing it really do to our society?
